      with 


      I went to the movie theater with Tom because I like being with him Fui al cine con Tom porque me gusta estar con él
      Mary is working with my brother. She is a nurse Mary está trabajando con mi hermano. Ella es enfermera
      Don't cut the bread with that knife. Try it with this one No cortes el pan con ese cuchillo. Inténtalo con este



      without 



      I cannot do my English homework without a dictionary. Do you have one? No puedo hacer mi tarea de inglés sin un diccionario. ¿Tienes uno?

      We cannot leave without Paul. Let's wait for a while No podemos irnos sin Paul. Vamos a esperar un rato

      It's not possible to trek that mountain without a guide No es posible escalar esa montaña sin un guía




      within 


      My plane is leaving within three hours. I'm going to the airport Mi avión se va dentro de tres horas. Estoy yendo al aeropuerto
      My friends will be here within an hour Mis amigos estarán aquí en una hora
      I don't have a pencil within reach. Can you wait a minute? No tengo un lápiz a mano. ¿Puedes esperar un minuto?



      across 



      They go to the school across the field Ellos van al colegio a través del campo

      Don't go across the highway, it's dangerous No vayas por medio de la autopista, es peligroso

      He ran across the field because it was late and he was scared Él corrió a través del campo porque era tarde y estaba asustado



      through 



      Mike and Jane go to town through the tunnel Mike y Jane van al pueblo a través del túnel

      I have planned my trip through an agency He planeado mi viaje a través de una agencia

      I go to my grandma's through the forest Yo voy a casa de mi abuelita a través del bosque

      They go dancing through the night Ellos van a bailar toda la noche

      She makes her mind up through her experience Ella toma las decisiones basándose en su experiencia

      We are walking through the woods because it's fun Estamos caminando a través del bosque porque es divertido

      We went through the gate and found that wonderful garden Pasamos a través de la puerta y encontramos ese maravilloso jardín

      Nota

      - across:Significa a través. Lo usamos para pasar a través de una superficie, no hay nada por encima.
      - through:Significa a través. Lo usamos cuando pasamos por medio de algo, puede haber algo por encima.
      We go to the bakery across the road Vamos a la panadería por la carretera
      Mike and Jane go to town through the tunnel Mike y Jane van al pueblo a través del túnel

    • Reading: Lee el texto detenidamente. Puede que en el texto figuren palabras que todavía no se han estudiado, para estos casos aconsejamos el uso de un diccionario. Después de leer el texto varias veces, responde las preguntas que figuran del mismo.


       Summer Camp

      A group of teenagers are listening to the instructions of their counselor. The instructions are really important because he is talking about an obstacle course. Each kid is pretty apprehensive about the course, because it looks really difficult. The kids have made up their minds that they will work together as a team to complete the course and show the counselor that they are not little children.

      Counselor: Okay, listen, kids! Today is the first day of the obstacle course. You must complete the course within 25 minutes. Each team will run the course with a white flag. At the end of the course, if there are any kids without a flag, your team will not continue through to the next course. Does everybody understand?

      Kid: What have we got to do?

      Counselor: Okay, first, you have got to swim across the lake, with your flag. Don't forget your flag! Then you have got to go across a wooden bridge. It is going to be difficult, but you can do it. Millions of kids have run this obstacle course. Then you have got to go through the tunnel on the other side and go up the hill. There is a small forest, that you must walk across. There is a gate on the other side. Go through the gate and you will see a post. When you arrive at the post, you must put your flag through the ring on the post. Are there any questions? No questions? Good. Let's get started then.
