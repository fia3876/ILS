    •   The Car Accident

      I was driving my old Renault through the city centre the other day when a lorry crashed into me! I got out of the car to talk to the lorry driver, a big dark looking man. He started to tell me that I was the cause of the accident. I did not want to fight with this scary man, so I called the police immediately. Thankfully, the police arrived inside of 10 minutes and began writing their report. Unfortunately, I had forgotten my drivers' license at home. I had stupidly put it on the bookshelf and then left for work! I knew then that this was going to be a nightmare. I explained to the policeman that the lorry driver had crashed into me and then wanted to fight with me. The policeman was a nice guy, a little bit young, but sympathetic until I told him that I did not have my driving permit. The policeman asked both drivers a lot of questions and then finished his report. He reported that the lorry driver caused the accident by crashing into me and has to pay 100 Euros; but I had forgotten my wallet at home and had to pay 150 Euros for driving without a license!
