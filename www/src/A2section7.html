    • Reading: Lee el texto detenidamente. Puede que en el texto figuren palabras que todavía no se han estudiado, para estos casos aconsejamos el uso de un diccionario. Después de leer el texto varias veces, responde las preguntas que figuran del mismo.


       The Mission


      A group of volunteers have come from all parts of the world to a small country in Africa. They have driven over 10 hours to reach this place. Most are engineers and have been to university, but some are students or construction workers. Nobody will be paid for the work they will do here, because they are here to help the people in the villages in this remote location. The people that live in this location have a difficult time when it rains. There are bridges that connect them with the towns where they do business, attend school and get medical treatment, but when it rains those bridges are destroyed by the river. The volunteers are here to build a new bridge for the people of the village. They have brought all of the materials and manpower necessary to complete their mission. This is not the first bridge to build. They have already built more than eight bridges in different parts of Africa. This mission started several years ago, but they have had to wait for the materials to build this particular bridge. Their first task is to build a camp for all of the volunteers to eat, sleep and work. They have brought enough supplies of food and water for the next few weeks. After that, they won't have any more food and water; therefore, they cannot waste any food or water while they are building the bridge. They have prepared a shower area for the women to use and one for the men. They used plastic and wood to build the showers. They did not drive these materials to the camp, they brought them on horseback and using donkeys. After they finish this bridge they will have to wait for the next bridge, which they will be building in Guatemala.
