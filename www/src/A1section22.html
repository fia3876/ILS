    • Grammar

    •   Preposiciones de Lugar II

      En lecciones anteriores hemos estudiado el uso de in, at y on cuando se refieren a lugar, veamos algunas preposiciones más:

      Under

      under debajo de
      The mouse is under the chair El ratón está debajo de la silla
      The pencil is under the backpack El lápiz está debajo de la mochila

      behind


      behind detrás de
      The movie theater is behind the sports center El cine está detrás del polideportivo
      There is a picture behind the table Hay un cuadro detrás de la mesa

      in front of


      in front of delante de
      My house is in front of the church Mi casa está delante de la iglesia
      The bird is in front of the window El pájaro está en frente de la ventana

      next to


      next to cerca de
      There is a drugstore next to the supermarket Hay una farmacia cerca del supermercado
      The man is next to the bus stop El hombre está cerca de la parada del autobús

      opposite


      opposite enfrente de
      The news stand is opposite the bakery El quiosco está en frente de la panadería
      My friends are opposite the school Mis amigos están en frente del colegio

      between


      between entre
      The park is between the movie theater and the post office El parque está entre el cine y la oficina de correos
      I'm between my brother and my father Yo estoy entre mi hermano y mi padre

      near


      near cerca de
      The horse is near the house El caballo está cerca de la casa
      I'm near the beach Estoy cerca de la playa

    •   Vacation in San Diego

       

      San Diego, California is a city in California. It has a perfect climate and many beautiful beaches. Today, Neil is in San Diego. He is on vacation. He is at the tourist information center. He wants information on events for the weekend. 

      Tourist information: Welcome to San Diego! 

      Neil: Thank You. I'm here this weekend. I want information about weekend events for the family.  

      Tourist information: The San Diego Zoo has a Fireworks display tonight. 

      Neil: My children are afraid of Fireworks. 

      Tourist information: Oh. There is a family night with games and prizes at the Hotel Del Coronado. The Hotel Del Coronado is next to the Coronado beach on the beach side of Coronado Island. Coronado beach is one of the top ten beaches in the United States. 

      Neil: Is family night tonight? 

      Tourist information: Yes. 

      Neil: Wonderful. There is the Coronado? 

      Tourist information: It is across the bay from us. 

      Neil: Are there any good restaurants? 

      Tourist information: Yes, and on the bay side of the island, every restaurant has a view of downtown San Diego. 

      Neil: How about tomorrow? 

      Tourist information: There is a dog show in Balboa Park. 

      Neil: My children love dogs. Is Balboa Park far from here? 

      Tourist information: Balboa Park is only two blocks from your hotel. Go to the show in the morning and then walk downtown and go to the Children's Museum or to the San Diego Padres baseball game. 

      Neil: Is there a baseball game tomorrow? 

      Tourist information: Yes. The San Diego Padres play the Los Angeles Dodgers at 3 P.M. Tickes are $12 at the front desk of your hotel. Inside Petco Park, there is an extra baseball diamond for children. 

      Neil: Thank you for your help.

    • Read the text and decide if the sentences are true (T) or false (F).
