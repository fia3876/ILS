    • Grammar

    •  For, during and while

      For: 
      Usamos for para hablar de la duración de una acción, cuanto tiempo dura esa acción.


      I have studied for four hours


      I have studied for four hours He estudiado durante cuatro horas
      Helen has studied Italian for two years Helen ha aprendido italiano durante dos años
      John has lived in France for five years now John ha vivido en Francia durante cinco años ahora

      During:
      Usamos during para decir cuando ocurre algo, no nos dice su duración.

      The accident happened during the night

      The accident happened during the night El accidente ocurrió durante la noche
      My mobile phone rang during the movie Mi teléfono sonó durante la película
      I fell asleep during the lesson Me quedé dormido durante la clase


      También podemos usar during en lugar de in para las partes del día.

      I was studying during the morning Yo estaba estudiando por la mañana
      I went to George's house in the afternoon Yo fui a casa de George por la tarde
      Kelly is having a party at night Kelly tiene una fiesta por la noche
      Kelly is having a party during the night Kelly tiene una fiesta durante la noche

      Nota

      during - durante
      Despúes de "during” siempre encontramos un nombre.
      while - mientras
      Despúes de "while” siempre encontramos una oración, es decir, un sujeto y un verbo.
      I got bored during the lesson Yo me aburrí durante la clase
      I got bored while the teacher was speaking Me aburrí mientras el profesor estaba hablando


      Ejemplos de uso

      Somebody knocked at the door while I was studying

      Somebody knocked at the door while I was studying Alguien llamó a la puerta mientras yo estaba estudiando
      The phone rang during the movie El teléfono sonó durante la película
      I have taught at that school for three years He enseñado en esa escuela durante tres años
      John and I have been friends for ages John y yo hemos sido amigos durante décadas
      I was watching TV while my father was working with the computer Yo estaba viendo la tele mientras mi padre estaba trabajando con la computadora

      I got hurt during the trip to the jungle

      Michael interrupted the teacher during his talk Michael interrumpió al profesor durante su charla
      Frank has travelled for four months now Frank ha viajado durante cuatro meses ahora
      I had breakfast while I was waiting for you Yo desayuné mientras te esperaba
      I lived in Rome for a year Yo viví en Roma durante un año

    •  The Grand Opening of a New Club


      Welcome, everybody to the Elite Club! We have an extravaganza here for you tonight that is truly amazing. Everything has been carefully planned for you by our Executive Chef Ronaldo Maldonado. You will see to the right of you a large selection of wines, which you can order during the evening. Our chef has prepared a wine tasting for you of the best wines from all around the world. You will find red wines from South Africa, New Zealand and Chile, also white wines from France, Spain and California. You can choose any wine you would like to taste and ask for a little in your glass. Next, while you are enjoying the wine, there is a lovely buffet of appetizers and snacks. The buffet is self service, so you can go directly there while you are waiting for the main course.

      While you are enjoying your snack and wine, the waiter or waitress will bring you a menu. The menu has special dishes prepared by the chef and a set meal, which is a little something that the chef has prepared especially for you. The set meal today is a prime choice filet mignon with rosemary roasted potatoes and a spinach and endive salad. There is no need to tip tonight, because all of our waiters and waitress are working without tips tonight; however, if you feel that the service was exceptional while you were here, please give the waiter a tip.

      Our restaurant is a full service restaurant, not fast food, and we also have a takeaway service, if you prefer to eat in the privacy of your own home.

      Well, thank you, ladies and gentlemen. I am glad that you are here and again, welcome to the Elite Club. If there is anything that you need during the evening, please find a waiter or waitress and they will help you. Enjoy your evening.
