    • Grammar

    •   Take + Noun + Preposition

      take advantage of - tomar ventaja de algo




      She took advantage of her beauty and got the job Ella sacó ventaja de su belleza y consiguió el trabajo
      She took advantage of her brother and got the car Ella sacó ventaja de su hermano y consiguió el coche
      He took advantage of his co-worker and got the promotion Él sacó ventaja de su compañero de trabajo y consiguió el ascenso


      take/ make profit of - sacar beneficios

      When he made the business deal, he took a profit Cuando hizo ese trato, él obtuvo ganancias
      The world crisis didn't affect the company. They made a profit last year La crisis mundial no afectó la compañía. Ellos tuvieron ganancias el año pasado
      My friend took a profit when he sold his car Mi amigo tuvo ganancias cuando vendió su coche
      He had to close the firm because it didn't make any profits Él tuvo que cerrar la empresa porque no obtenía ganancias


      take offence at - ofenderse



      I had to apologize to Joan. She took offense at my comment last night Me tuve que disculpar con Joan. Ella se ofendió anoche con mi comentario
      I didn't take offense with you. I just wasn't happy that night Yo no me ofendí contigo. Simplemente no estaba feliz esa noche
      Mike took offense with you because you were rude to him Mike se ofendió contigo porque fuiste grosero con él


      take part in - tomar parte en algo 

      take place - transcurrir, ocurrir






      The demonstration took place in front of the World Trade Center La manifestación ocurrió en frente del World Trade Center
      I didn't go with you because I didn't want to take part in the parade No fui contigo porque no quería tomar parte del desfile
      Terry didn't take part in the fight because he is a very peaceful person Terry no tomó parte en la pelea porque él es una persona muy pacífica


      take care of - tener cuidado de, cuidar




      She took care of me when I was sick Ella me cuidó cuando estaba enferma
      I'm taking care of my sister tonight Cuidaré a mi hermana esta noche
      You don't need anybody to take care of you. You are a grownup No necesitas que alguien cuide de ti. Tú eres un adulto

    • Tourism Update

      With the recent global economic problems, tourism has taken a new turn and many people are taking advantage of it. If you can manage to take a holiday, you may be one of the lucky few. Research shows that the percentage of the global population that can take a holiday to a destination outside their own country is much less than in previous years. But this is good news for those people who are able to have a holiday and travel abroad. There are many great deals out there for tourists. Many tour operators are taking less of a profit in order to generate business and tourists should take advantage of this opportunity. Ask your travel agent about a package holiday to optimize savings; many holiday resorts are offering discounts.

      Ecotourism is more and more popular every year and can be a great option if you would like to try something unique and different. Because ecotourism is a relatively new business, you can also find deep discounts in certain locations that will help your wallet. You do not have to stay in an eco resort to try out an ecotour. Ecotours can be arranged by contacting the tourist information office in the area you are visiting. Ecotours are great for children because they get the chance to see animals in their own habitat and meet indigenous people in their villages, all of which is truly an educational experience for the kids and well worth the price.

      While hotels and tour operators offer deep discounts, the same is not true of the airlines. Not only are the airline fares going up, but lots of new charges are appearing. For example, before the recession, the airlines allowed you to take 2 to 3 bags with no charge. Recent changes in many airlines policies have been taking advantage of the passengers and charging passengers for excess baggage, which is considered to be more than one bag, the new baggage allowance. You will not find great discounts with the airlines because they are not taking part in discount deals. Airline officials say that they have been badly affected by fuel prices and other factors.

      If you are not one of the fortunate people who can afford to travel abroad, why not travel in your own area? A good way to save money on your holiday is to tour around a historic place near your own home. Many historic cities and towns offer walking tours, complete with a street map that shows you all of the historic places and what the best route is. These walking tours are very popular in some historic places, such as Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, where walking tours often look like a parade.

      Speaking of parades, try to visit tourist destinations during their holidays because many places have a parade in honor of the holiday. Parades are free and a great way to spend an afternoon with the kids.
