    • Grammar

    •   Writing a biography

      Para escribir una biografía ordenamos los hechos más importantes en orden cronológico y utilizamos expresiones típicas.

      Ejemplos: 

      to be born - nacer

      Es una estructura pasiva que todavía no hemos estudiado, pero es fácil de comprender. Conjugamos el verbo to be en pasado "was / were” y entendemos la palabra "born” como un adjetivo.

      George Frank was born in California on June the tenth of nineteen thirty two George Frank nació en California el diez de Junio de 1932
      His father was a farmer and his mother was a nurse in a doctor's surgery Su padre era granjero y su madre era enfermera en la consulta de un médico


      to start school - empezar el colegio


      His sister, Lilly, was three years old when she started school Su hermana, Lilly, tenía tres años cuando empezó el colegio
      He started school in nineteen thirty four at the age of three Él empezó el colegio en 1934 a la edad de tres años
      He finished middle school and went to high school. He played basketball at high school Él terminó el Colegio y fue al Instituto. Él jugó al baloncesto en el Instituto


      to go to university - ir a la universidad


      He went to university at the age of eighteen and studied law Él fue a la universidad a la edad de dieciocho años y estudió Derecho


      to get married - casarse

      Ocurre lo mismo que en el caso de"to be born”. Se trata de una estructura pasiva que entenderemos de momento como el verbo get + adjetivo, de manera que conjugamos el verbo "to get” en cualquier tiempo y añadimos la palabra "married” como si se tratase de un adjetivo.


      He got married in nineteen fifty two at the age of twenty-five Él se casó en 1952 a la edad de 25 años
      His wife, Jule Smith, was studying physics at university Su mujer, Jule Smith, estaba estudiando Física en la universidad
      They both finished university in nineteen fifty four and moved to New York Ambos terminaron la universidad en 1954 y se mudaron a Nueva York


      to get a job - conseguir un trabajo


      He got a job as a lawyer in an important firm in New York and he became a well-known lawyer in the city Él consiguió un trabajo en una importante empresa en Nueva York y llegó a ser un conocido abogado en la ciudad
      Jule got a job at the university. She became a teacher and a researcher Jule consiguió un trabajo en la universidad. Llegó a ser profesora e investigadora


      to have a child - tener un hijo


      They had a child in nineteen fifty-seven and they named him Robert Ellos tuvieron un hijo en 1957 y le llamaron Robert
      George established his own company in nineteen sixty-two, and it was very successful George creó su propia compañía en 1962 y tuvo mucho éxito
      They moved to a better house in nineteen sixty five Ellos se mudaron a una casa mejor en 1965
      They had a second child in nineteen sixty four and her name was Sheila Ellos tuvieron una segunda hija en 1964 y su nombre fue Sheila
      The family travelled around the world for a year in nineteen sixty-nine La familia viajo alrededor del mundo durante un año en 1969
      They were a happy and well-known family Ellos fueron una familia feliz y conocida
      The Franks had their first grandson in nineteen eighty-four Los Franks tuvieron su primer nieto en 1984

    •   Obituary for Harrison Sosa


      Harrison Sosa, the successful businessman, died in his sleep last night at the age of 85. He was one of the city's richest and most powerful men for years. He was born in 1921, in Manchester. By the time he was 20, he had already made his first million. By 1976, he had bought 8 small companies and established them as successful businesses. When he was 30, he married his wife, June. He and June never had any children. They later divorced and after the divorce, he became the youngest multimillionaire in the country. For the last ten years he has not gone out of his house much and has spent most of his time with his sisters. Memorial services will be on Sunday at 1:00 p.m.
