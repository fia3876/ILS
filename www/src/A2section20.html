    • Grammar

    •  Wait , Expect and Hope

      wait - esperar 

      expect - esperar 

      hope - esperar 


      wait for 

      Usamos este verbo cuando nos referimos a esperar físicamente:



      I was waiting in the train station for an hour but he didn't arrive Estuve esperando en la estación de tren durante una hora pero él no llegó
      I'm waiting for your brother; he is taking a shower Estoy esperando a tu hermano; se está dando un baño
      Can you wait in the living room, please? ¿Puede esperar en la sala, por favor?
      Mr. Park was waiting for you in your office, Mr. Grand El señor Park le estaba esperando en su oficina, señor Grand


      Nota

      Es frecuente utilizar la expresión can't wait cuando queremos que algo ocurra pronto:

      I can't wait to see the pictures of our travel No puedo esperar para ver las fotos de nuestro viaje
      John can't wait to go to the party John no puede esperar para irse a la fiesta
      The children can't wait to eat the cake Los niños no pueden esperar para comerse el pastel


      expect

      Usamos este verbo cuando esperamos que algo ocurra según nuestras expectativas:



      I expect to pass my Spanish test tomorrow morning Espero aprobar mi examen de español mañana por la mañana
      They expect his friend to agree with him Ellos esperaban que su amigo estuviese de acuerdo con él
      The boss expected the sales to go higher that month El jefe esperaba que las ventas subieran ese mes
      I don't expect to finish my degree; I'm working too hard to pay for it Yo no espero terminar mi carrera; estoy trabajando muy duro para pagarla


      hope

      Usamos este verbo para hablar de lo que esperamos a un nivel emocional, de nuestras esperanzas:



      I hope Tom will be happy in his new house Espero que Tom sea feliz en su nueva casa
      My mom hopes that I will finish university Mi madre espera que yo termine la universidad
      Ruth hopes to work abroad, but she doesn't have a job yet Ruth espera trabajar fuera, pero ella no tiene un trabajo todavía
      I hope it's good weather in Edinburgh, we're spending the weekend there Espero que haga buen tiempo en Edimburgo, vamos a pasar el fin de semana allí


      Ejemplos de uso


      A man is waiting outside the house. Do you know him? Un hombre está esperando fuera de la casa. ¿Lo conoces?
      Mark expects his grades to be good this year. He has worked very hard Mark espera que sus calificaciones sean altas este año. Él ha trabajado muy duro
      My sister hopes to get married to her boyfriend soon Mi hermana espera casarse con su novio pronto

      I expect to be free by five o'clock. Can you wait for me? Espero estar libre a las cinco. ¿Me puedes esperar?
      John hoped to work as a teacher, but he didn't finish his degree John esperaba trabajar como profesor, pero él no terminó su carrera
      I waited for my friend for a long time and then he called to tell me he was sick Esperé a mi amigo mucho rato y entonces me llamó para decirme que estaba enfermo
      I was hoping my ex-boyfriend would go to the party, but he didn't Yo esperaba que mi ex novio viniese a la fiesta, pero no vino

    •  Travel Agency

      Stan and Jan are at the travel agency making their travel plans. They will be going to a Caribbean island and they are hoping that the weather is nice for their holiday. Sasha is their travel agent, and they are waiting for her to find the brochures about the hotel that they will be staying in. 

      Sasha: I am back with the brochures. Please have a look. There are some guided tours that I can recommend. Will you be buying travel insurance? 

      Stan: I do not think we'll need it. 

      Jan: Are you sure, honey? Remember last year when our luggage was lost on the luggage trolley? 

      Stan: You are right. I guess we will probably need it. 

      Sasha: Now, a few important things. You will be travelling to the Caribbean where the sun is quite strong. I am sure you are going to be sunbathing. I highly recommend that you use suntan lotion with a sun block so that you do not get sunburnt. The objective is to have a nice healthy suntan, not a sunburn! 

      Jan: That is a good idea. I have been waiting all winter to have some sunshine and I do not want to ruin my holiday! 

      Stan: To be honest, I was hoping to spend some time in the casino! 

      Jan: I will be sunbathing while you are spending time in the casino. I hope there is a spa in the hotel. 

      Sasha: Yes, there is a lovely spa. I can make an appointment for you. 

      Jan: That sounds nice. 

      Sasha: I will call them and they will be expecting you. 

      Jan: Brilliant! I want to do some exploring of the island and some shopping too. Is there a duty free shop near the hotel? 

      Sasha: Yes, there is a duty free shop. You can expect to pay much better prices in the duty free. I hope you have a very nice holiday. 

      Stan: Thank you. I am sure we will.
