    •   Futuro Perfecto interrogativo

      Para formar la interrogación con esta forma verbal colocamos el auxiliar en posición inicial:

      Will
      		sujeto
      		have
      		past p.
      		complementos ?

      		a shower in an hour ?
      Will
      		Moira
      		have
      		learned
      		Russian by August ?
      Will
      		your sister
      		have
      		finished
      		her homework before nine o'clock ?


      Will you have had a shower in an hour?

      Will you have had a shower in an hour? ¿Te habrás dado una ducha en una hora?
      Will Moira have learned Russian by August? ¿Habrá aprendido Moira Ruso en Agosto?
      Will your sister have finished her homework before nine o'clock? ¿Habrá terminado tu hermana su tarea antes de las nueve?
      Will Nathan have arrived by Monday? ¿Habrá llegado Nathan el lunes?
      Will Doctor Hugh have left the hospital by half past one? ¿Habrá dejado el doctor Hugh el hospital a las una y media?
      Will my friend have passed his test? ¿Habrá aprobado mi amigo su examen?

      When will you have left home?

      When will you have left home? ¿Cuándo te habrás ido de casa?
      Why will they have gone by half past seven? ¿Por qué se habrán ido ellos a las siete y media?
      How long will Frank have travelled by next week? ¿Durante cuánto tiempo habrá viajado Frank la semana que viene?
      When will you have returned? ¿Cuándo habrás vuelto?
      Why will Jude have bought a car? ¿Por qué habrá comprado Jude un coche?
      What will you have learned when you finish the course? ¿Qué habrás aprendido cuando termines el curso?
      Why will you have talked to Louis? ¿Por qué habrás hablado con Louis?

      Ejemplos de uso


      How will you have gotten home? ¿Cómo habrás llegado a casa?
      Will you have found a job by Tuesday? ¿Habrás encontrado un trabajo el martes?
      Will they have made a decision by Friday? ¿Habrán tomado ellos una decisión el viernes?
      Who will have gone with you? ¿Quién habrá ido contigo?
      Will your parents have moved to Cambridge? ¿Se habrán mudado tus padres a Cambridge?
      What will you have played in the concert? ¿Qué habrás tocado en el concierto?
      Will Daniel have brought his guitar? ¿Habrá traído Daniel su guitarra?
      When will Frank have arrived in France? ¿Cuándo habrá llegado Frank a Francia?

    • Reading

    • Reading: Lee el texto detenidamente. Puede que en el texto figuren palabras que todavía no se han estudiado, para estos casos aconsejamos el uso de un diccionario. Después de leer el texto varias veces, responde las preguntas que figuran del mismo.


       The Arrival of a Princess

      Princess Pollyanna is coming for a visit and she will be a guest at an event in the hote where I am working. This is a spectacular event and everything should be absolutely perfect for the Princess. I am meeting with the organizer of the event, Robert Evans, to check the program of the event and finalize the plans.


      Robert: The princess will be arriving in a few hours. How is everything going?

      Me: Everything is completely on time.

      Robert: I don't see any flowers. Where are the flowers?

      Me: The flowers will have been delivered and set on the tables before the Princess arrives.

      Robert: What about the champagne? Has it been put in the refrigerator?

      Me: Not yet. But it will have been cold for an hour by the time she gets here.

      Robert: This is all making me very nervous. Will it be cold before she gets here?

      Me: Yes, don't worry. It will have been in the fridge for an hour and a half before the Princess gets here.

      Robert: I need the buffet prepared before she gets here. Will the buffet have been prepared by 4:00 p.m.?

      Me: Yes, the buffet will have been prepared before she arrives.

      Robert: Is the choir here yet?

      Me: Not yet. They are quite a distance away, but they are on their way. They will have already started to play by 4:00 p.m. Everything is under control, Robert. I know that it appears that we are not yet organized, but believe me everything will have been finished by the time the Princess arrives.

      Robert: I hope so.
