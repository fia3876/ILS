    • Grammar

    •  Health words



      surgery - cirugía


      His patient will have surgery today. He has a pain in his leg Su paciente tendrá cirugía hoy. Él tiene mucho dolor en su pierna
      I am having brain surgery as they need to remove a tumor from there Hoy tendré una cirugía en el cerebro ya que necesitan remover un tumor de ahí


      family doctor - médico de cabecera


      I wasn't feeling good so my mom called the family doctor Yo no me estaba sintiendo bien así que mi madre llamó al médico de cabecera
      Sue had an accident with her bike, so she went to the family doctor yesterday Sue tuvo un accidente con su bicicleta, así que fue al médico de cabecera ayer


      contagious - contagioso


      I had a tropical illness last year; and it was terrible, but it wasn't contagious Tuve una enfermedad tropical el año pasado y fue horrible, pero no era contagiosa
      I don't want to sit next to you because you have a cold that is contagious No quiero sentarme contigo porque tienes un resfriado que es contagioso


      mumps - paperas


      I didn't have the mumps when I was a child, I was a lucky boy Yo no tuve paperas cuando era niño, fui un niño afortunado
      Rachel has the mumps, she is at home and her face is awful Rachel tiene paperas, ella está en casa y su cara está horrible


      midwife - comadrona


      A midwife was helping my mom with the birth, she was a nice woman Una partera estaba ayudando a mi madre en el parto, era una mujer agradable
      My sister is a midwife, she works at the hospital Mi hermana es partera, ella trabaja en el hospital


      antiseptic - antiséptico


      Don't worry Mrs. Adams, it's nothing serious. I'm going to give you some antiseptic for the infection No se preocupe Señora Adams, no es nada grave. Le voy a dar unos antisépticos para la infección
      I used some antiseptics because I cut my hand while I was cooking Estoy usando unos antisépticos porque me corté la mano mientras estaba cocinando


      X-ray - rayos X


      The doctor wants to take an X-ray to see my arm El doctor quiere hacerme una radiografía para ver mi brazo


      measles - sarampión


      My brother has the measles, his face is full of red spots Mi hermano tiene sarampión, su cara está llena de puntos rojos
      John has been all the week with the measles, he is bored John lleva toda la semana con sarampión, él está aburrido

      indisgestion - indigestión


      I ate too much last night and I had terrible indigestion Comí demasiado anoche y tuve una indigestión horrible
      I don't want to eat much. I have indigestion and stomach pain No quiero comer mucho. Tengo indigestión y dolor de estómago

    •  A hospital

      Rain is just beginning to fall as I sit on our sailboat on the bright blue sea. We are having a long holiday and having a travel to the islands. It is paradise to live in the islands, but there are many things that you have got to take into account. Today, I am thinking about those things because I have been looking after my young daughter Samantha for 2 days. She is sick and I know that I have to find a good hospital for her, because we do not have our family doctor here. 

      Most of the hospitals here are good for typical tropical diseases or indigestion, but not if you need surgery. I am hoping that is not the case for Samantha. I find the thermometer next to the antiseptic in the bathroom and walk into her room to take her temperature. I see that she has a light fever. I fear it is pneumonia, mumps or measles. I am beginning to worry about her as I prepare to leave for the next island. There isn't a doctor on this island. I find a small, clean clinic with a friendly nurse. I am starting to feel better about the situation. The nurse takes Samantha to have an examination by the doctor. 

      Within minutes a tall, kind looking man comes in, he tells me my daughter is fine and has the measles; but measles are contagious and she will have to stay in the hospital for 1 week in order to not contaminate other people and start an epidemic. I go home to pack my stuff and prepare to spend a week in the hospital with Samantha.
