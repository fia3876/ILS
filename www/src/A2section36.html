    • Reading


    • Reading: Lee el texto detenidamente. Puede que en el texto figuren palabras que todavía no se han estudiado, para estos casos aconsejamos el uso de un diccionario. Después de leer el texto varias veces, responde las preguntas que figuran del mismo.


      Protecting the President

      It is not easy to protect the president, but that is my job. I work for the government in the"Antiterrorist Unit.” The antiterrorist unit is responsible for investigating and stopping all terrorists. We are the police of the terrorist world and we attend antiwar demonstrations to spy on terrorists. Now, you have no idea how much work is necessary and the responsibility that we have in order to protect the president. First of all, every year there is new technology that we can use, so frequently the limousine of the president is rebuilt with better protection. There are special rooms in government buildings that are also rebuilt or remodeled not to be more stylish, but to besafer for the president. People always want the president's autograph, and we have to give him extra protection in those situations. That can be the most dangerous situation for us, because the president is vulnerable when he is giving autographs. Ex-presidents also have protection; although, after they are an ex-president all they do is write their autobiography! The protection for ex-presidents is funded by the government, and they do not have to repay the funding.


      Lee el texto y decide si las frases son verdaderas (T) o falsas (F).
