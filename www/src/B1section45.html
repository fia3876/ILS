    • Grammar

    •   Collocations

      lose one's temper- perder los nervios



      I lost my temper when he told me such a big lie Perdí los nervios cuando me contó esa mentira tan grande
      She lost her temper because she is nervous Ella perdió los nervios porque está nerviosa
      I'm not losing my temper. I'm calm No estoy perdiendo los nervios. Estoy tranquila


      get away with it- escaparse impunemente


      The thief broke into the house, and he got away with it El ladrón entró en la casa, y se escapó impunemente
      I fought with my brother last night. I'm punished, but he got away with it Me peleé con mi hermano anoche. Estoy castigada, pero él se escapó impunemente
      I have failed five subjects; but my father is very busy and I've gotten away with it He suspendido cinco asignaturas, pero mi padre está muy ocupado y me he escapado impunemente


      pay attention (to someone or something)- prestar atención


      Pay attention to the man you see on the screen Prestad atención al hombre que veis en la pantalla
      Pay attention to me, please. I'm talking seriously Préstame atención, por favor. Estoy hablando en serio
      You never pay attention to the teacher, so you fail all the time Nunca le prestas atención al profesor, así que suspendes todo el tiempo


      make sure- asegurarse



      Make sure you have all your stuff before we leave Asegúrate de que tienes todas tus cosas antes de irnos
      I made sure I had my driver's license, but I cannot find it now Me aseguré de que tenía mi carnet de conducir, pero no puedo encontrarlo ahora
      Have you made sure the door is closed? ¿Te has asegurado de que la puerta esté cerrada?


      get into trouble- meterse en líos



      My brother got into trouble with the police because he was too drunk Mi hermano se metió en un lío con la policía porque estaba muy borracho
      I fulfill my duties because I don't want to get into trouble Cumplo mis obligaciones porque no quiero meterme en líos
      She got into trouble because she was on drugs Ella se metió en un lío porque se drogaba

    •   Are you a candidate to be self-employed?

      Everybody wants to be their own boss and manage their own operations, but it is not as easy as you think. Self-employed people must not be shoddy workers because it is obvious who did the shoddy work. In order to be self-employed, you have to be ready to work casual labour, which means that you may not have work sometimes. Casual labour can sometimes be a good experience, especially if you are an apprentice or unskilled, because you can learn new skills and talents that can help you on future jobs. However, self-employed people need to pay attention to their work twenty-four hours a day. If you do not pay attention, you can get into trouble very quickly. It is your responsibility to make sure that everything is finished when it should be finished. You should also make sure that there are no errors in the work. You must keep any licences that you have current and not let them expire. 

      An office worker can get away with doing less work because there is always somebody else that can help complete the work. They know that the office manager will pay them even if their work is shoddy. Sales assistants can sometimes get away with not doing a lot of work because they receive commissions and they depend on sales to get those commissions. Some salespeople receive a small amount of pay plus commissions. Many of them are satisfied with the pay and are not motivated to sell anything; however, this is a losing attitude because you cannot get away with that forever. One day your boss will pay attention to the number of sales and he will ask to leave the company. 

      Administrative assistants have the most difficult jobs, and the highest skills. They have a lot of responsibility and they can't even get away with an extra fifteen minutes for lunch! Because they are administrators, people are always looking for them for advice. They must be prepared for anything that the boss wants if they do not want to get into trouble. They have to make sure that everything is perfect so that the boss doesn't lose his temper.
