    • Grammar

    •  Distances and dimensions

      broad / broaden

      broad ancho
      broaden ampliar


      New York has broad and long avenues Nueva York tiene avenidas anchas y largas
      History is a broad subject. I study it carefully Historia es una materia amplia. Yo la estudio cuidadosamente
      My parents have broadened the living room. It was very small before Mis padres han ampliado la sala. Era muy pequeño antes
      I have studied a course on international relations because I want to broaden my job expectations He estudiado un curso en relaciones internacionales porque quiero ampliar mis expectativas de trabajo


      wide / width / widen

      wide ancho, amplio
      width anchura, amplitud
      widen ampliar
      My bedroom is three meters long and five meters wide Mi habitación tiene tres metros de largo y cinco metros de ancho
      The width of my bedroom is five meters El ancho de mi habitación es de cinco metros
      My parents are widening the house, they are building a garage Mis padres están ampliando la casa, ellos están construyendo una cochera
      My room is wider than my brother's room Mi habitación es más ancha que la de mi hermano
       

      Wide es más común que broad

      tall 

      Normalmente lo usamos para personas, aunque a veces podemos usarlo para cosas siempre que esa cosa sea alta y más bien delgada, puede ser un edificio, un árbol ...

      tall alto


      That was the tallest tree I have ever seen, it was amazing Ese fue el árbol más alto que yo he visto jamás, era sorprendente
      John is taller than me, but I don't mind John es más alto que yo, pero no me importa
      The Empire State is a really tall building in New York El Empire State es un edificio realmente alto de Nueva York


      high 

      Sólo lo usamos para cosas

      high alto, elevado


      Everest is a really high mountain, but I want to climb it El Everest es una montaña realmente alta, pero yo quiero escalarla
      There are some really high towers in that city, it's a medieval city Hay algunas torres realmente altas en esa ciudad, es una ciudad medieval
      That plane was flying really high, man. I think it's dangerous Ese avión estaba volando realmente alto, tío. Yo creo que es peligroso


      deep / depth / deepen

      deep profundo, hondo
      depth profundidad
      deepen profundizar


      They were diving deep in the sea, they said it was wonderful Ellos estaban buceando profundo en el mar, ellos dijeron que era maravilloso
      Susan took a new job. It has deepened her love for her career Susan tomó un nuevo trabajo. Ha profundizado su amor por su carrera
      The cave was deep in the mountain, I wanted to come back La cueva era profunda en la montaña, yo quería volver
      The depth of the swimming pool is about two meters La profundidad de la piscina son unos dos metros
      Our travel around Europe deepened our love for each other Nuestro viaje por Europa profundizó nuestro amor el uno por el otro
      That unlucky accident has deepened her sadness about life Ese desafortunado accidente ha profundizado su tristeza por la vida

       My Secret Place


      There's a beautiful place that I like to go to when I need some peace and quiet. This place gives me a sense of tranquility. It's a place where the deep blue sea meets the highest mountains that you have ever seen. It is a place that deepens my sense of the world and makes me content to be a part of it. This place is very special, from its tall trees with their broad leaves and the sunshine making them a golden color like a dream. My place has no tall buildings or wide avenues with traffic. My place is full of Earth's creatures and beauties. I think that the depth of the emotions that I feel in this place really broaden my horizons and permit me to be a better person. 

      Where is this place? I cannot tell you because it is my secret place. If I tell you, it will not be a secret anymore.
