      below



      The temperature in New York is below zero during the winter time La temperatura en Nueva York es debajo de los cero grados durante el invierno
      There is a river below the bridge Hay un río debajo del puente
      The water level of the river is below normal because we had a drought this year El nivel del agua del río está por debajo de lo normal porque tuvimos una sequía este año


      "Under” versus "Below”

      Ambas significan"debajo de”, pero:
      Usamos below para referirnos a un nivel inferior.
      Joan lives in the apartment below Joan vive en el departamento de abajo
      Usamos under para referirnos a un mismo nivel.
      Your shoes are under the bed Tus zapatos están debajo de la cama

      beyond



      The price of that car is beyond what I can afford El precio de ese coche está más allá de lo que puedo pagar
      The drugstore is located beyond the bridge on the right hand side La farmacia se localiza más allá del puente, en tu lado derecho
      Don't stay out beyond midnight tonight No te quedes fuera más allá de la media noche
      Beyond the mountain is a large forest Más allá de la montaña hay un extenso bosque

       Top Class Service

      In a top class restaurant or hotel it is very important to give the best possible service to the customers. Customers usually have higher expectations of service when the restaurant is known to be top class and believe that the service staff should go above and beyond their expectations. That is exactly what well-trained service staff does.

      The Ritz-Carlton is a prime example of a hotel whose staff goes above and beyond the expectations of their guests by constantly surprising them and delighting them with excellent Ritz-Carlton service. The Ritz-Carlton has excellent training for their staff and they make sure that the service is never below an acceptable level. When you walk into a Ritz-Carlton Hotel it appears that everything is calm and organized and the surprising thing is that every member of the staff knows your name immediately when you come in. You are greeted by your name at the reception, in the restaurant and even in the gift shop. How do they do it? The Ritz-Carlton has an intricate labyrinth of what happens behind the scenes. The name of each guest is passed to the staff, along with a description of the person. Each member of the staff is expected to memorize the name and description of the person so that they are able to greet the person by name. There is a lot of work that goes on behind good service.

      In a restaurant, one of the most important aspects of giving first class service is to never get behind. That is true for the kitchen, too; because if the chef gets behind on preparing the food, the waiter will get behind on serving the food. An excellent service staff may get behind, but they work together to catch up and not get behind again. One of the most important things is that the restaurant is not understaffed. When a restaurant is understaffed, even by one or two waiters or chefs, the service will be below par

      When people stay in a luxury hotel or eat at a luxury restaurant, their expectations are much higher because they are paying above what they would pay in a mid-range restaurant or hotel. Just as the old saying goes, you get what you pay for; people want more when they pay more. So if your job is to give service in a high-class restaurant or hotel, you should go above and beyond what would be good service and give your customers excellent service.
