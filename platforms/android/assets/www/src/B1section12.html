    •  Must in the negative form


      As you learnt before, the verb must has got different uses. There are different ways to make its

      negative form. Lets learn them. 

      Must Not or Mustn't 

      We use it when it involves a moral duty. As you already know it means está prohibido.



       
       

      You mustn't take a shower before 8:00 Está prohibido bañarse antes de las 8:00

      You must take a shower before 8:00 Debes bañarte antes de las 8:00


      Don't have to

      It is used when we want to say no tener que. This form is more common that "mustn't".




      I don't have to buy any food for tomorrow's trip No tengo que comprar comida para la excursión de mañana
      I must buy some food for tomorrow's trip Debo comprar algo de comida para la excursión de mañana


      Cannot

      We use it when it involves a logical deduction. As you already know it means es imposible, no puede ser.




      This university cannot be hard Esta universidad no puede ser difícil
      This university must be really hard Esta universidad debe ser realmente difícil



      Examples:


      I must work hard if I want to get that promotion Debo trabajar duro si quiero obtener ese ascenso
      I don't have to work hard tomorrow No tengo que trabajar duro mañana
      You must be in your room early at night Debes estar en tu habitación temprano por la noche
      You mustn't be out late at night Está prohibido que estés fuera hasta tarde por la noche
      It must be an old car Debe ser un coche viejo
      It cannot be an old car No puede ser un coche viejo
      He has passed his test, he must be happy Él ha aprobado su examen, debe estar contento
      He has passed his test, he cannot be disappointed Él ha aprobado su examen, no puede estar decepcionado
      You must get up before 8:00 in the morning Debes levantarte antes de las 8:00 de la mañana
      You mustn't get up after 8:00 in the morning Está prohibido levantarse después de las 8:00 de la mañana
      You must tidy your room every day Debes ordenar tu habitación todos los días
      You don't have to tidy your room every day No tienes que ordenar tu habitación todos los días

       The Weight Loss Program

      Five women have started a program to go on a diet and lose weight. Each woman has promised to lose 35 kilos. Of course, they have no idea what they must do to lose the weight. Their weight loss assistant is going to explain the rules and regulations of the weight loss program.

      Assistant: Okay, everybody. You are all here in order to lose weight. The amount of weight that you must lose is a minimum of 35 kilos. Of course, you don't have to lose more than that, but many people go on to lose even more. Many people think that because this is a program, it must be easy. There is no easy weight loss program. They are all difficult, but you can be successful and take off those kilos. You will have to observe all of the rules and regulations, however.

      Rule number 1: You must eat all of your meals here in the center. You cannot eat anything that is not allowed. This is a very important rule. You must eat what the doctors have recommended for the diet. You do not have to be hungry because the diet has all kinds of delicious food, but it is nutritious food that is balanced with the vitamins and minerals that your body needs for maximum energy. You must have energy because you must exercise, too.

      Rule number 2: You may leave the center for only 15 minutes each day, to visit friends and family. You cannot eat anything during those visits. You must be back in your room immediately after the visit

      Rule number 3: You must weigh yourself every day in order to see the progress. Most participants say: What? I did not lose any weight? That cannot be! Sometimes it takes time to start to lose weight. So do not get depressed if you do not see much change in the first few days. You must be patient.

      Okay. It is 10:00 a.m. and we have not had breakfast yet. You must be hungry. So let's begin with our first meal together.
