    • Grammar

    •  Futuro continuo afirmativo y negativo

      Para formar el futuro continuo de cualquier verbo utilizamos el verbo auxiliar"will” + el verbo "to be" en base + el verbo que conjugamos en -ing:

      Sujeto
      		will
      		be
      		verb
      		complementos
      I
      		will
      		be
      		having
      		a holiday next month
      My parents
      		will
      		be
      		travelling
      		to Rome tomorrow morning
      You
      		will
      		be
      		sleeping
      		when I arrive
      They
      		will
      		be
      		playing
      		football this time next week




      I will be having a vacation next month Yo me estaré tomando unas vacaciones el mes que viene
      My parents will be travelling to Rome tomorrow morning Mis padres estarán viajando a Roma mañana por la mañana
      You will be sleeping when I arrive Tú estarás durmiendo cuando yo llegue
      They will be playing soccer this time next week Ellos estarán jugando al fútbol a esta hora la semana que viene
      I will be meeting my friends on Friday afternoon Yo me estaré viendo con mis amigos el viernes por la tarde
      My brother will be teaching in a school when he finishes his degree Mi hermano estará enseñando en una escuela cuando termine su licenciatura

      Para hacer su forma negativa negamos el auxiliar:

      I will not be living with my parents next month Yo no estaré viviendo con mis padres el mes que viene
      You will not be studying tomorrow night Tú no estarás estudiando mañana por la noche
      They will not be watching TV later Ellos no estarán viendo la tele más tarde
      We will not be travelling to Cork this weekend Nosotros no estaremos viajando a Cork este fin de semana


      Nota

      Puedes contraer la partícula "not” con el auxiliar:
      will not = won't

      I won't be flying to Denmark this week Yo no estaré volando a Dinamarca esta semana
      She won't be studying in the summer Ella no estará estudiando en verano
      The cat won't be running throughout the house El gato no estará corriendo por toda la casa



      Ejemplos de uso:



      Don't call me between half past two and three, I will be driving home No me llames entre las dos y media y las tres, estaré conduciendo a casa
      I will be teaching in a high school when I finish university Estaré enseñando en un instituto cuando termine la universidad
      You won't be driving home tonight, you have drunk too much Tú no estarás conduciendo a casa esta noche, tú has bebido mucho
      Ruth won't be coming to the party, she isn't in town Ruth no vendrá a la fiesta, ella no está en la ciudad
      We will be having lunch together on Friday Nosotros almorzaremos juntos el viernes
      My friends won't be having a party on Friday night. They have some tests next week Mis amigos no estarán haciendo una fiesta el viernes por la noche. Ellos tienen algunos exámenes la semana que viene


      I will be having tea with Jude on Friday afternoon Estaré tomando té con Jude el viernes por la tarde
      I won't be studying tonight, I will be watching TV No estaré estudiando esta noche, estaré viendo la tele

    •  Future Transportation

      Scientists and entrepreneurs say that by the year 2050, we will be using different methods of transportation. Some say that we will be driving electric cars; others say that we will be using space technology as transportation. All experts agree that the world will not be depending on petrol by the year 2050. 

      Some speculate that we will be travelling through the air in the future. Imagine that! We will not be making one way streets anymore! And what about speed limits? I guess we won't be having a speed limit with this future transportation. I think that we will still be needing car parks, however. 

      Apparently we will be having holidays on the moon by the year 2050; in fact, even sooner, because a hotel will be opening on the moon in the next 10 years. Of course, we will not be driving to the moon; we will be taking a special rocket ship that Sir Richard Branson will be completing soon. 

      It is all very exciting, but I wonder about pedestrians. If you want to walk to your destination, will that be possible?
