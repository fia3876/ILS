    • Reading

    • Reading: Lee el texto detenidamente. Puede que en el texto figuren palabras que todavía no se han estudiado, para estos casos aconsejamos el uso de un diccionario. Después de leer el texto varias veces, responde las preguntas que figuran del mismo.


       I need a new job!


      It is nice to be employed, but at the same time some bosses and managers are completely inconsiderate. My new boss is so unfriendly that it makes me uncomfortable. When it is convenient for her, we leave the office earlier than usual. But when I have something very important to do, she makes me stay at the office late. It is so inconvenient that I cannot simply ask my boss to leave a little bit early, but she is so unkind about my personal situations. There have been a few unbelievable times, like that time she didn't let me leave on a Saturday for my son's soccer game! It was so unfair. My work was not incomplete, but she told me that it was impossible for me to leave early. Sometimes my boss makes me so unhappy. I don't say anything because I do not want to be impolite, but I am honestly starting to dislike the situation. After all, I am not incompetent; in fact, I am the best secretary she has ever had! Well, I guess I just do not want to be unemployed. But I am a very qualified person, so maybe looking for a job is not an impossible task. It is important for a worker to be satisfied with your job and with your boss. Although in this modern world it is not unusual for a worker to be dissatisfied. Maybe I just disagree with the way bosses are so insensible.
