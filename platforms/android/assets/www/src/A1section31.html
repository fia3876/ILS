    • Grammar

    •   Usos del Presente Simple

      Utilizamos el presente simple para:

      1-Expresar rutinas, lo que es habitual en nuestra vida, la realidad inamovible a la que nos remitimos, lo que siempre es

      igual:


      I get up at seven o'clock every day Yo me levanto a las siete todos los días

      She plays tennis on Tuesday Ella juega tenis los martes

      My mother goes shopping once a week Mi madre va de compras una vez a la semana

      2-Horarios de medios de transportes, de la televisión. Normalmente se organizan por temporadas, por lo tanto es algo

      constante.


      I go to work on the half past five train Yo voy a trabajar en el tren de las cinco y media

      My father watches his favorite sports program at quarter to nine every day Mi padre ve su programa favorito de deportes al cuarto para las nueve cada día

      3-Verdades generales: Las cosas que nunca cambian, que siempre son así:


      When it rainy, the floor gets wet Cuando llueve, el suelo se moja

      The sun in the morning Por la mañana sale el sol

    •   Verbos Estáticos

      Además de los usos que hemos estudiado, hay un grupo de verbos que siempre deben ser conjugados en forma simple y nunca en forma

      continua, hablamos de los verbos estáticos.

      1-Pensamiento:


      think creer, pensar

      decide decidir

      believe creer, aceptar como verdad

      know saber, conocer

      consider considerar

      remember recordar

      Understand entender

      He believed in god Él cree en dios

      I consider your opinion Yo considero tu opinión

      2-Sentimientos:


      love amor

      like gustar

      enjoy divertirse, disfrutar

      dislike desagradar

      hate odiar

      want querer

      hope esperar, confiar en

      prefer preferir

      I love History A mí me encanta la Historia

      She hates apple Ella odia las manzanas

      John wants an orange juice John quiere un juego de naranja

      3-Verbos de percepción involuntaria de los sentidos:


      see ver

      touch tocar

      smell oler

      hear oír, escuchar

      taste probar, saber

      That cake smell sweet ese pastel huele dulce

      I hear a nice song Yo oígo una canción agradable

      The soup taste good la sopa sabe bien

      4-Medidas :


      wigh pesar

      You weigh fifty-five kilos Tú pesas 55 kilos

      The table height is one meter La mesa mide 1 metro

      My cat weigh three kilos Mi gato pesa 3 kilos

      5-Posesión:


      belong to pertenecer a

      have tener, posser

      She has long, blonde hair Ella tiene el pelo largo y rubio

      I have three brothers and two sisters Yo tengo tres hermanas y dos hermanos

      This car belongs to my father Este coche pertenece a mi padre

      Cuando estudiemos el presente continuo, haremos una comparación de estos verbos con otros que si pueden ser expresados en forma

      continua.

    •   A sunny day


      Hello friend,

      I hope are you well. I live in New Jersey in  a two story house. It has four bedrooms and two bathrooms. I have my own room. I live down on the street from a park. It is a very nice neighborhood. I have two cats and a bird. My cats' names are Tiger and Buster. My town is small and has many stores. Right now, it is hot out and very sunny. There are many things to do, such as go to the movies, the mall, and restaurants. My family is big, I have 3 sister and two brothers, I live with my parents and Grandma. My two sisters also lives with me. My oldest sister works in a bank. She is very happy. I go to the college and have a job working with computers.

      in my free time I like to read books, watch television and go to the park. I also like to have fun with my friends. During the summer, i like go to the beach and have swim. I like to ride my bike and walk around the town. When I have a holiday, I like to travel to different places around the world. It is a good learning experience. When you go to different countries you learn amy things about their culture.

      Please send me a letter back to tell me about you.

      Thank you!

      Sophia
