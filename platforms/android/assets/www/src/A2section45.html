    • Grammar

    •   Phrasal Verbs: fill up, find out, hand up, look over

      US: fill out 

      UK: fill up - rellenar un formulario



      He filled out his application form and gave it to the secretary Él llenó su solicitud y se la dio a la secretaria
      Please, fill out your name and address on the top of the page Por favor, ponga su nombre y dirección en la parte de arriba de la página
      I've filled out all the questions on the test; I think I have passed it He contestado todas las preguntas del examen; creo que he aprobado

      find out - averiguar
       
        
      He found out the whole truth about his wife's affair. He found her love letters Él averiguó toda la verdad del amorío de su esposa. Él encontró sus cartas de amor
      I have found out a way to study quicker He averiguado una manera de estudiar más rápido
      He found out everything because you told him Él lo averiguó todo porque tú se lo contaste
       

      look over - examinar
       
        
      The teacher looked over our homework and said we should work harder El profesor examinó nuestra tarea y dijo que deberíamos trabajar más duro
      The police officer looked over my driver's license and said it was right El policía examinó mi licencia de conducir y dijo que estaba bien
      Mom looked over my essay and gave me some advice Mamá examinó mi redacción y me dio algunos consejos
       

      Hang up       - colgar (put something on a hanger or receiver)

      Why did you hang the phone up while I was speaking? ¿Por qué colgaste el teléfono mientras estaba hablando?
      You can hang your coat up in the hall Puedes colgar tu abrigo en la entrada
      I hung my hat up and entered the living room Colgué mi sombrero y entré en el salón

        The Job Application

       

       Oscar: Mum! Where are you? I am home. 

      Mum: I am here. I am talking on the phone. 

      Oscar: Please hang up the phone. I need some help filling up this job application. 

      Mum: A job application? Brilliant! Charlene, I have got to go. I have got to help Oscar fill up a job application. I will call you back later. 

      Oscar: Thanks mum. Now, the first thing that I need is the correct address where we live.

       Mum: Okay, that is 401 Nottingham Road, postal 33389. Let me see the application for a minute. Well, there is a wide variety of information that you have got to fill in on this form. Why don't you have a go at it and when you are finished, I will look it over to see if there are any errors. 

      Oscar: But it looks difficult! 

      Mum: Oscar, it is just an ordinary job application. You need experience in filling these up. Once you fill up the whole form, I will help you with anything you need. 

      Oscar: Okay, mum. You are right, I need experience filling these up. 

      Mum: So Oscar, what kind of job is it? 

      Oscar: I am not sure. I am going to find out when I take the application form back; but they told me it is working as a salesperson.

      Mum: Sales? What are you selling? 

      Oscar: Thingamajigs.
