    •   Frases hechas

      for sale - en venta


      I want a house. I saw a very nice one last week and I think it's for sale


      I want a house. I saw a very nice one last week and I think it's for sale Yo quiero una casa. Vi una muy bonita la semana pasada y creo que está en venta
      Look! That car is for sale. Weren't you looking for one? ¡Mira! Ese coche está en venta. ¿No estabas buscando uno?
      The house isn't for sale. My parents don't want to sell it La casa no está en venta. Mis padres no quieren venderla


      to give someone the cold shoulder - Comportarse de una forma poco amigable hacia alguien



      I met George at the party, I think he's angry with me, he gave me the cold shoulder


      I met George at the party, I think he's angry with me; he gave me the cold shoulder Me encontré a George en la fiesta, creo que está enfadado conmigo; me ignoró
      My teacher gave me the cold shoulder when I asked him about the test Mi profesor me ignoró cuando le pregunté por el examen
      We were planning the weekend, but Mark gave the cold shoulder to all our ideas Nosotros estábamos planeando el fin de semana, pero Mark no estaba entusiasmado con ninguna de nuestras ideas


      to be in a hurry - ir con prisa

      I wanted to have a tea with you but my boss has phoned me and now I'm in a hurry


      I wanted to have tea with you, but my boss has phoned me and now I'm in a Quería tomarme un té contigo, pero mi jefe me ha llamado y voy con prisa
      It's a quarter past four and our train leaves at half past four! Come on! We're in a hurry ¡Son las cuatro y cuarto y nuestro tren sale a las cuatro y media! Vamos ¡Tenemos prisa!
      I was in a hurry because I was late for school Yo tenía prisa porque llegaba tarde al colegio


      to be in love with somebody - enamorarse de alguién



      I'm in love with Sue but I don't know how to tell her


      John was in love with me when we were at school, but I didn't like him John estaba enamorado de mí cuando estábamos en el colegio, pero a mí no me gustaba
      I'm in love with Sue, but I don't know how to tell her Estoy enamorado de Sue, pero no sé cómo decírselo
      They are going out together because they are in love Ellos están saliendo juntos porque ellos están enamorados
      I was in love with Frank last year, but now I'm not Yo estaba enamorada de Frank el año pasado, pero ahora no lo estoy


      to throw cold water on something - echar agua fría sobre algo / quitar entusiasmo acerca de algo



      I studied hard and I thought my test was good but my teacher threw cold water on it when he told me the mark


      I wanted to go to that university, but my parents didn't like the idea and they threw cold water on it Yo quería ir a esa universidad, pero a mis padres no les gustaba la idea y me quitaron las ganas
      They wanted to go abroad for a vacation but Ron didn't like the idea and threw cold water on it Ellos querían ir al extranjero de vacaciones pero a Ron no le gustaba la idea y les quitó las ganas
      I studied hard and I thought my test was good; but my teacher threw cold water on me when he told me the grade Yo estudié duro y pensaba que mi examen estaba bien; pero mi profesor me echó agua fría cuando me dijo la not


      on second thought - pensándolo dos veces

      We wanted to go camping but we weren't sure about the weather so on second thoughts we decided to go to a hostel


      We wanted to go camping, but on second thought, we decided to go to a hostel Queríamos ir de camping, pero después de pensarlo dos veces, decidimos ir a un albergue
      They wanted to buy a house but it was really expensive, so, on second thought, they decided to move to a different one Ellos querían comprar una casa, pero era realmente cara, así es que pensándolo dos veces, decidieron mudarse a otra
      I wanted to go to university but I also wanted to get money, so, on second thought, I decided to get a job Yo quería ir a la universidad, pero también quería conseguir dinero, así es que pensándolo dos veces, decidí conseguir un trabajo

    • Reading: Lee el texto detenidamente. Puede que en el texto figuren palabras que todavía no se han estudiado, para estos casos aconsejamos el uso de un diccionario. Después de leer el texto varias veces, responde las preguntas que figuran del mismo.


        Long Distance Relationship


      When Tom met Sally, he was living quite a distance from her house; in fact, about an hour away. At the time he did not think about the distance and he and Sally drove to be together every weekend. Sally had a beautiful house that she had bought years before. She really loved the house. It was spacious and was decorated to be comfortable and cozy; in fact, Tom also loved the house. He thought it was a great place to spend a weekend with a pretty girl. 

      But then he and Sally fell in love and started to think about getting married. The distance between their houses threw cold water on the love relationship, and Tom started to have second thoughts about Sally and their relationship. Tom and Sally talked about breaking up, but they decided that the relationship was solid and that they really were in love. Sally agreed that the best solution to the problem was to sell her house. She put the house on the market; however, the market was slow that time of year. The house was for sale for six months, but nobody bought it. They were tired of waiting for a buyer. They were in a hurry to sell the house and get married! 

      Sally felt like Tom was giving her the cold shoulder during the times that they spent together. She eventually asked him about it and he said that he was having second thoughts about the relationship. Sally was sad, but if she lost Tom, the love of her life, she would still have her house that she loved. Sally and Tom broke up. Sally fell in love with her neighbor and she did not sell her house. She and the neighbor lived happily together in the house.
