    • Grammar

    •   So and Such

      Ambas palabras pueden significar"tan” pero las usamos de forma diferente. 

       Usamos so + adjetivo o adverbio

      It's so cold Hace tanto frío
      It's so quick Es tan rápido
      She is so nice Ella es tan agradable


      Usamos such + a/an + adjetivo + nombre singular


      It's such a cold day Es un día tan frío
      It's such a quick car Es un coche tan rápido
      She is such a nice girl Ella es una chica tan agradable



       

      Nota

      Como puedes observar puedes hacer la misma frase con ambas partículas cambiando el orden de los elementos.
      Observa la posición del artículo "a/an” en ambas oraciones.

      También podemos usar such + adjetivo + nombre plural

      They were such old houses Eran unas casas tan viejas
      We ate such delicious cakes Nos comimos unos pasteles tan deliciosos
      They have such beautiful horses Ellos tienen unos caballos tan bonitos
         

      Nota

      Observa que al ser el nombre plural o incontable, no necesitas usar el artículo "a/an”


      Podemos usar ambas partículas como intensificadoras del significado de un adjetivo

      He passed the exam because he studied so hard Él aprobó el examen porque estudió muy duro
      The weekend was great. It was so much fun to ride a horse El fin de semana fue fantástico. Fue realmente divertido montar a caballo
      I'm learning history; it's such an interesting subject Estoy aprendiendo Historia; es una materia tan interesante

      We enjoyed our meal. We had such a nice talk Nosotros disfrutamos de nuestra comida. Tuvimos una charla muy agradable
      The kids enjoyed their trip. It was such nice weather at the beach Los niños disfrutaron de su excursión. El clima fue tan agradable en la playa
      We had such a fun party last night Tuvimos una fiesta realmente divertida anoche


      Ejemplos de uso


      I like the castle, it's so old Me gusta el castillo, es un castillo tan viejo
      I love your sister. She's such a pretty girl Me encanta tu hermana. Es una chica tan guapa
      They enjoyed their vacation. It was such an exciting vacation Ellos disfrutaron sus vacaciones. Fueron unas vacaciones tan emocionantes
      I don't understand your friend, he speaks so quickly Yo no entiendo a tu amigo, él habla muy rápido
      Edward finished his degree with such good grades Edward terminó su carrera con unas notas realmente buenas
      Mom cooked tuna for dinner last night, it was so tasty Mamá cocinó atún para cenar anoche, estaba muy sabroso
      John behaved great last night; he is such a polite boy John se comportó muy bien anoche; él es un chico tan educado
      I don't want to talk about it because it's such a terrible situation No quiero hablar de eso porque es una situación tan horrible

    • Reading: Lee el texto detenidamente. Puede que en el texto figuren palabras que todavía no se han estudiado, para estos casos aconsejamos el uso de un diccionario. Después de leer el texto varias veces, responde las preguntas que figuran del mismo.


       Such a Beautiful Day

      Darwin O'Neal and Sheila Franklin are getting married today. Both the bride and the groom are quite wealthy and the wedding has been planned for over a year. They are so famous and wealthy that more than 4,000 people will attend the wedding! The castle that they are renting for the wedding is such an old castle that there isn't any electricity. Everything will be decorated with candles, just like the Tudor times! There will be so many candles in the halls, in the bathrooms, in the dining room! The Duchess of Kent will be attending. She is so old that she usually falls asleep at public functions, but she is such a nice lady that nobody cares.

      The families of the bride and groom have both spent so much money that you cannot believe it! They have bought so many flowers and so much food that it will be a grand wedding. The bride's dress is so beautiful; covered with pearls and silver beads. The dress is really heavy because there are so many pearls on it! The bride is not so pretty, but the dress is so beautiful that nobody will notice.

      Since there are so many guests, the chef has ordered over 500 bottles of champagne. He does not usually order so many, but this is a special occasion and it will leave such good memories.

      It is such a beautiful day and there are so many people getting ready for this special event. I am so late, that I need to hurry!


      Read the text and decide if the sentences are true (T) or false (F).
