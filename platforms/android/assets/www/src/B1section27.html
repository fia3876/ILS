        Why you should donate blood


      The Red Cross is one of the biggest blood banks in the world; but it is not the only one. Besides the Red Cross, there are many other blood banks where you can donate blood. What can you expect as a blood donor? First, there is a blood test. The blood test is to check if your blood is safe to donate. An analysis of your blood is necessary to see if you have any diseases or other health problems. Besides good health, the other requirements are that you have a healthy body weight and that you do not have high blood pressure. People that want to donate blood do not need to check in to the hospital, because they are outpatients. Depending on if you are donating blood or blood plasma, somebody will be monitoring you during the process of giving blood. It is not dangerous to donate blood and if you are nervous, you can bring a friend to sit beside you while you are giving blood. You cannot donate blood every day, however.

      There are many good reasons to donate blood, besides the obvious ones. If you know that you are going to have surgery, you can donate blood towards the blood that you will need for the surgery. In other words, by donating your own blood, the blood will be a credit towards whatever blood you will be using from the blood bank.

      Remember that the process is safe and sterile and there is nothing to worry about. The patient is put in a quiet room and the doctor will put the receptacle for the blood beside the patient. The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes, during which you will be seated comfortably. When they have taken approximately a pint of blood, the donation is complete and a staff person will place a bandage on your arm. After donating, you should have a snack and something to drink. They will give you orange juice and cookies. You can leave after 10-15 minutes and continue with your normal daily activities.

      People ought to donate blood because it is very important that the blood banks have sufficient blood to give to patients that need it. Besides, you can have the feeling that you have helped save lives by donating blood.
