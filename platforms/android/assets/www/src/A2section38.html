    • Grammar

    •   Easily confused words

      love - amar, encantar

      love amar, gustar

      I love my boyfriend. I want to marry him

      I love my boyfriend. I want to marry him Amo a mi novio. Me quiero casar con él
      I love jazz. It's a relaxing type of music Me encanta el jazz. Es un tipo de música relajante


      adore - adorar

      adore adorar

      John adores his mum, he is always with her

      John adores his mom, he is always with her John adora a su madre, él siempre está con ella
      Kathy adores her children; she is always taking care of them Kathy adora a sus hijos; ella siempre los está cuidando

      worship - adorar a un dios



      worship adorar

      They worshipped their God with several ceremonies

      They worshipped their God with several ceremonies Ellos adoraban a su Dios con varias ceremonias
      Celtic people worshipped the forces of nature Los celtas adoraban a las fuerzas de la naturaleza


      revere - reverenciar


      revere reverenciar

      The government revered the work of this writer in an official event

      Most people revered the work of this writer La mayoría de las personas reverenció el trabajo de este escritor
      The two brothers revered their grandfather Los dos hermanos reverenciaron la memoria de su abuelo

      idolize - idealizar

      idolize idolatrar

      They idolized U2, they went to all their concerts

      They idolized U2; they went to all their concerts Ellos idealizaban a U2; iban a todos sus conciertos
      My sister idolizes Pearl Jam, she loves this band Mi hermana idealiza a Pearl Jam, a ella le encanta este grupo


      hate - odiar


      hate odiar

      I hated girls when I was a child


      I hated girls when I was a child Odiaba a las niñas cuando era niño

      I hate my sister, she is always annoying me Odio a mi hermana, siempre me está molestando

      dislike - no gustar


      dislike tener antipatía a , desagradar

      I dislike apple pie, I prefer carrot pie, please


      I dislike apple pie. I prefer carrot cake, please No me gusta el pastel de manzana. Prefiero el pastel de zanahoria, por favor

      I dislike this movie because they are killing somebody all the time No me gusta esta película porque ellos están matando a alguien todo el tiempo

      detest - detestar


      detest detestar

      My brother detests his history teacher, he says he is very serious


      My brother detests his history teacher. He says he is a very serious person Mi hermano detesta a su profesor de Historia. Él dice que es una persona muy seria

      I detest Bob. He always wants to be first Detesto a Bob. Siempre quiere ser el primero

    •   Coldplay

      Ali: Do you like Coldplay, Sandra?

      Sandra: I love Coldplay! I adore all of their songs. Why do you ask?

      Ali: They're giving a concert here next month. Would you like to go?

      Sandra: I would love to go! I really like Chris Martin's voice because it's amazing. I cannot believe he is married to Gwyneth Paltrow!

      Ali: From what I have seen, they are a great couple and he worships her.

      Sandra: And she adores him. They look like they are totally in love.

      Ali: What other bands do you like? Do you like Radiohead?

      Sandra: I do not like them very much. It is not that I dislike them, they are just not my favorite band. Hey, do like that crazy singer, Lady Gaga?

      Ali: No, I hate her. Her music is not bad, but she thinks that everybody should idolize her. I don't know, I do not like her because she annoys me.

      Sandra: It is the same for me with Britney Spears. I hate her because she thinks she is adorable and so talented. I don't like her music at all.

      Ali: What about the classic David Bowie?

      Sandra: David Bowie! I love his music. I love that song"Heroes” that he sings.

      Ali: Oh, yes, I love that song!

      Sandra: What day is the concert?

      Ali: It is the 16th, it's on a Sunday.

      Sandra: Oh, no! I cannot go on a Sunday. I have got to spend time with my grandmother.

      Ali: That's too bad because it is going to be a great concert.

      Sandra: I love my grandmother, I just hate that I have got to visit her every Sunday.

      Ali: I know what you mean.

    • Read the text and decide if the sentences are true (T) or false (F).
