      My friend lives near the train station Mi amigo vive cerca de la estación de tren
      Your interest is near mine in this business Tu interés está muy cerca del mío en este negocio
      I live near a big park past the Church Vivo cerca de un gran parque pasada la iglesia


      past

      I arrived home past midnight yesterday Llegué a casa pasada la medianoche ayer
      The museum is past the university El museo está pasando la universidad
      Our French test is at half past six Nuestro examen de francés es a las seis y media


      along


      I go along the High Street to go to work Camino a lo largo de la calle principal para ir a trabajar
      I walk along the High Street every day to go to work Yo camino a lo largo de la calle principal cada día para ir a trabajar
      Walk along that road and turn right. You will find the Church around the corner Camine a lo largo de esta carretera y gire a la derecha. Encontrará la iglesia al girar la esquina
      I don't like to walk along the path on Sundays. I like to rest on Sunday No me gusta pasear a lo largo del camino los domingos. Me gusta descansar los domingos
      I walk along the main road to go to school every day Yo camino a largo de la calle principal para ir al colegio cada día


      about


      There are about fifty boys in the playground Hay unos cincuenta chicos en el patio de recreo
      They are talking about history Ellos están hablando de Historia
      I haven't known anything about him since the last time he visited us No he sabido nada de él desde la última vez que nos visitó
      I wanted to learn more about my studies, but I didn't have a clue about how to do it Yo quería aprender más sobre mis estudios, pero no tenía pista de cómo hacerlo

        Letter from Herbert


      Dear Mr. Craken: 

      I understand that you will be coming here next week to look at the new location for your boutique. I think I have the perfect location for you. This place will bring high traffic to your boutique; and during the tourist months, about forty thousand people will be travelling to the city! 

      You probably do not know about this place because it has not been on the market very long, but people are already starting to talk about it. The location is not in the city centre, but it is very near the city. There is also a bus route by the shop, about three or four streets down. Many people walk along this street because there are many cafes and small restaurants near the bus route. 

      As you enter the city, you go along the main avenue, about ten minutes by foot. Go left on Yard Street. You will see the buses travelling along the avenue. There is a large church near the avenue. You will continue along the avenue about two more minutes, and you will see a little shop past the Bistro Café. 

      If you would like to know more about this location, please ring me up at 332-4956. 

      Sincerely 

      Herbert Manchester
