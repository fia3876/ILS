       Ocala: Horses, Nature and Culture in Florida


      When people think of Florida, they think of Disney World and Sea World. There are many other cities in Florida with their own attractions. Ocala Florida is one of these other great cities in Florida. It has horses, a theme park, nature and culture.


      There are a lot of different horse farms located in Ocala. If you are interested in working on a horse farm, this is the place to come. If you want to learn to ride horses, you can also find that in Ocala also. There are also well know horse sculptures placed in different places in Ocala and little souvenirs in the shops around town. 

      Nature can be found in different places in Ocala. Silver Springs theme park is located here. It is a theme park with animals, springs, and nature. There are also many different parks and springs located in and near Ocala. Any outdoor activity you would enjoy can be found here. 


      The down town square in Ocala often has different events through out the year including art fairs, dance presentations, a festival of lights at Christmas, and other fun events to bring people together. Ocala has a few different theaters including a community theater where anyone from Ocala can audition to be part of the plays. 

      So when you are thinking of Florida, don't just think of Disney World, think of horses, nature and culture. There are many other great cities in Florida to visit and Ocala is one of the many choices to visit.
