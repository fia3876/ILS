        A Reunion with My Hometown


      I just returned from my hometown. I have not been there for 12 years or more. I woke up this morning in the same bed that I had when I was a child, and I smelt the breakfast that my mum was making. There is nothing like when you wake up in your mother's house and she is preparing breakfast!

      How it has changed over the years. Every street, every shop that I passed had a memory. I started remembering everything the first day, passing by old friends that I had not seen for ages. I even saw Stephen, my ex-boyfriend. I remember how handsome he was in High School! It seems like yesterday. We had a tea together in the village and he told me how I had broken his heart and he had never recuperated from that. I had no idea! I never meant to break his heart or hurt him in any way. I was quite surprised. All these years I had never thought about it. I thought that it was a reciprocal breakup!

      Then I went by the floral shop to choose some flowers for my mother's kitchen table. The flowers that I chose were her favorites, violets. My mum had had violets in her gardens for years, but she does not have a garden anymore. I thought that the violets were a good choice. I meant to get some cakes, too, but I forgot. I walked home, thinking about Steve and what had happened to us.

      When I got home, Stephen was there, talking to my mum. It seems that mum had seen Stephen in the village, too, and had invited him to dinner!
