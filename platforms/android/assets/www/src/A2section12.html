      Verbo to write


      write escribir
      wrote escribir (past)
      written escribir (past p.)
      I am writing a postcard to my cousin right now Estoy escribiendo una postal a mi prima ahora mismo
      Jane wrote lots of letters to me when she was in China Jane me escribió muchas cartas cuando estaba en China
      Did your brother write to you last week? ¿Te escribió tu hermano la semana pasada?
      I have written a postcard to my cousin because she wanted to see the city Le he escrito una postal a mi prima porque ella quería ver la ciudad
      I haven't written to my family because I haven't had time No le he escrito a mi familia porque no he tenido tiempo


      Verbo to see

      see ver
      saw ver (past)
      seen ver (past p.)
      I saw Mike yesterday, but he didn't see me Vi a Mike ayer, pero él no me vio
      John saw some beautiful birds in the beach yesterday John vio algunos pájaros bonitos en la playa ayer
      I saw you in the park last week, but I didn't say anything because I was in a hurry Yo te vi en el parque la semana pasada, pero no dije nada porque tenía prisa
      I have seen wild animals in the zoo He visto animales salvajes en el zológico
      They have seen Peter, but they haven't talked to him Ellos han visto a Peter, pero no han hablado con él
      I haven't seen your bag, where did you put it yesterday? Yo no he visto tu mochila, ¿dónde la pusiste ayer?


      Verbo to spend

      spend gastar, emplear
      spent gastar, emplear (past)
      spent gastar, emplear (past p.)
      I spent the summer in Belgium because I wanted to travel Yo pasé el verano en Bélgica porque quería viajar
      My cousins spent the weekend with me, it was quite fun Mis primos pasaron el fin de semana conmigo, fue bastante divertido
      I spent three hundred dollars on a new bike, it wasn't expensive Gasté 300 dólares en una nueva bicicleta, no era cara
      I have spent all morning in the library, I have a test tomorrow morning He pasado toda la mañana en la biblioteca, tengo un examen mañana
      My sister has spent a lot of money on her new car, but she really wanted it Mi hermana se ha gastado mucho dinero en su coche nuevo, pero realmente lo quería
      My friend has spent the weekend in Galway, he has some friends there Mi amigo ha pasado el fin de semana en Galway, él tiene algunos amigos allí


      Verbo to know

      know saber, conocer
      knew saber, conocer (past)
      known saber, conocer (past p.)
      I didn't know anything about the matter, so I didn't say a word Yo no sabía nada del asunto, así que no dije nada
      John knew you were coming so he waited for you John sabía que tú venías así que te espero
      I knew all the answers to the test so I think I have passed Yo sabía todas las respuestas del examen así que creo que he aprobado
      I have known the answer all the time He sabido la respuesta todo el tiempo
      They have known each other since they were at school Ellos se han conocido desde que estaban en el colegio
      Have you known about George? He has had an accident ¿Has sabido lo de George? Él ha tenido un accidente


      Verbos regulares

      He considered the idea, but he didn't agree with them Él consideró la idea, pero no estaba de acuerdo con ellos
      I believed in ghosts when I was a kid Yo creía en fantasmas cuando era niño

      Mr. Brown learned to drive last year. He thought it was more difficult Mr. Brown aprendió a conducir el año pasado. Pensó que era más difícil
      I studied for a degree in philosophy at the university, now I'm a teacher Yo estudié la carrera de Filosofía en la universidad, ahora soy profesor
      I washed my car yesterday, it was very dirty Yo lavé mi coche ayer, estaba muy sucio

    •  Sorry I Haven't Written!

      Dear Mum, 

      I am so sorry that I have not written to you in ages! I think the last time I wrote you was about 2 weeks ago. It is not that I haven't been thinking about you, it is just that we are enjoying our trip through Europe so much! I learned a lot about Europe in my school lessons, but there are many things that I did not know about the cathedrals, for instance. I have never seen such beautiful churches and cathedrals in all of my life. They are the most spectacular that I have seen. We have been spending a lot of time in the art galleries, too. Not so much the museums where you will find Picasso, but the small art galleries where you will find little known artists who are very good, but not famous yet. 

      We haven't spent all of our money yet! So you need not worry that I am writing to you to ask for money. We have been staying in small hostels in each city. The hostels are fine because we do not spend much time in our room, as we are out and about trying to see everything in the city! 

      I have not seen the church that you suggested I see yet. But we will be spending some time in that area next week, so I am sure we'll see it them. 

      Well, that's all for now. We will see you next month at home. 

      Take care and I love you 

      Cynthia
