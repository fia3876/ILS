    • Grammar

    •  Adjectives followed by prepositions

      Algunos adjetivos van seguidos de una preposición concreta. Para poder usarlos debes familiarizarte con ellos: interested in - interesado en





      My father is interested in History, he knows a lot about it Mi padre está interesado en la Historia, sabe mucho sobre eso
      I am not interested in your life, Sue. Don't tell me more stories about your boyfriends No estoy interesada en tu vida, Sue. No me cuentes más historias sobre tus novios
      John is interested in languages, he speaks five languages John está interesado en los idiomas, él habla cinco idiomas



      aware of - consciente de




      They weren't aware of the dangers of the jungle Ellos no eran conscientes de los peligros de la selva
      Mike isn't aware of his attractive look. All the girls like him Mike no es consciente de su aspecto atractivo. A todas las chicas les gusta
      The students aren't aware of the difficulty of the test Los estudiantes no están conscientes de la dificultad del examen


      famous for       - famoso por






      Oscar Wilde is famous for his ironical and comical plays Oscar Wilde es famoso por sus obras irónicas y cómicas
      The south of Spain is famous for its delicious food and wonderful beaches El sur de España es famoso por su deliciosa comida y sus maravillosas playas
      Belgium is famous for its chocolate Bélgica es famosa por su chocolate



      to be good at - ser bueno en
      to be bad at - ser malo en







      John is really good at math, he always gets high grades John es realmente bueno en Matemáticas, él siempre saca calificaciones altas
      My brother is very bad at English, he always needs some help Mi hermano es muy malo en inglés, él siempre necesita ayuda
      My friends are very good at basketball. They are playing for a professional team now Mis amigos son muy buenos en baloncesto. Ellos están jugando en un equipo profesional ahora



      worried about - preocupado por







      I'm worried about my sister, it's very late and she hasn't come back home yet Estoy preocupada por mi hermana, es muy tarde y no ha vuelto a casa todavía
      My father is worried about my future. He wants me to go to university Mi padre está preocupado por mi futuro. Él quiere que vaya a la universidad
      I'm worried about my test; I think I haven't studied enough Yo estoy preocupado por mi examen; creo que no he estudiado suficiente



      different from - diferente de 






      London is quite different from New York, but I love both cities Londres es bastante diferente de Nueva York, pero me gustan ambas ciudades
      My book is different from yours. Is it a new edition? Mi libro es diferente del tuyo. ¿Es una nueva edición?
      My car is different from yours. Yours is more fashionable, but mine is faster Mi coche es diferente del tuyo. El tuyo es más moderno, pero el mío es más rápido

        Advice for the Future


      Ben's father always gives Ben good advice about life and what he should do with his future. His father never insists that Ben take the advice, he allows Ben to make the final decision. Ben is good at many things. He is good at science, chemistry and maths, and he thinks he will be a medical care professional, but he is not sure. Ben does not usually worry about his marks in school, because he always gets good mark and does not even have to do his homework! His parents do not force Ben to do his homework because they know that his marks are excellent.

      The medical profession seems like a logical choice for Ben, but his father recommends that he be aware of all of his choices. His father says that it is important to try to be different from the rest of the people. He says that it isn't really important to do something that you will be famous for, but something that will make you happy and that you are good at.

      For now, Ben isn't worried about his decision because he is only 16 years old; but he knows that it is important to be aware of the choices that exist in the professional world.
