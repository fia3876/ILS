      in


      There is something to eat in the kitchen Hay algo para comer en la cocina
      Steve wasn't in the office, I think he is sick Steve no estaba en la oficina, creo que está enfermo


      He has a house in Boston Él tiene una casa en Boston
      In the afternoon we sat in the park because it was a sunny day Por la tarde nos sentamos en el parque porque era un día soleado
      I wasn't in town yesterday; I was visiting a friend in Cork No estaba en la ciudad ayer; estaba visitando a un amigo en Cork

      into


      He ran into the room and fell down Él entró corriendo a la habitación y se cayó
      Samuel became very selfish when he found that job, he turned into a monster Samuel se volvió muy egoísta cuando encontró ese trabajo, él se convirtió en un monstruo


      The rainy weather changed into a beautiful sunny day El tiempo lluvioso cambió a un bonito día soleado

      inside


      They were inside the room, but they didn't have the key Ellos estaban dentro de la habitación, pero no tenían la llave
      Mike is at the hospital, he is visiting his niece Mike está en el hospital, él está visitando a su nueva sobrina


      The bird isn't inside the cage, where is it? El pájaro no está en la jaula, ¿Dónde está?
      I ran a marathon and my time was just inside 4 hours Corrí una maratón y mi tiempo estuvo justo dentro de las 4 horas
      The kid was inside the closet because he was playing with his sister El niño estaba dentro del armario porque estaba jugando con su hermana

      Observa: in vs inside

      I am taking the train in an hour Yo tomo el tren dentro de una hora


      I am taking the train inside an hour Yo tomo el tren en menos de una hora


      outside


      The boys were outside the school, they were waiting for the bus Los chicos estaban fuera del colegio, ellos estaban esperando el autobús
      There is a huge wall outside the castle; it isn't possible to go in Hay una enorme muralla fuera del castillo; no es posible entrar
      There is a big garden outside the house, the kids love it Hay un gran jardín fuera de la casa, a los niños les encanta
      There is a wonderful forest outside the village Hay un bosque maravilloso fuera del pueblo
      I was out of town because I wanted to see the forest Estuve fuera del pueblo porque quería ver el bosque

    •  Bad Dreams

      When I was a little kid, I always had bad dreams. Sometimes the dream was so bad that it was a nightmare. Of course, like all little kids, my dreams usually included monsters. I remember one dream very clearly, even to this day. 

      There was a scary forest outside a village, and for some reason I was there. There was a wall around the village and the village was inside a wall, so it was not possible to get in. I was outside the village, in the forest. Of course, in dreams there is always nonsense, things that are not sensible or logical. I was walking through the forest and I saw some deer in the forest. They did not look like ordinary deer to me. Inside of a minute, they had changed into monsters! The monsters were enormous and they began looking directly at me! 

      I needed to get inside the wall, where the village was. I knew that I was safe in the village. I began running like I was running a marathon. Then I woke up! I was not in the forest, I was not in the village, I was at home in my bed. Just to be sure, I checked the wardrobe to see if there were any monsters inside.
